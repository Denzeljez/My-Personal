Hello! I’m Denzel Jezreel Lazo, an IT student and entrepreneur from Barangay Nalasin, Santo Domingo, Ilocos Sur. I come from a family of six, and we’re all proud members of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. As the second eldest sibling, I value family deeply, and I’m grateful to be married, having been sealed in the Philippines Manila Temple—a milestone that means a lot to me.

Crafting is my outlet for creativity and relaxation. I specialize in motorcycle seat modifications and restorations, transforming worn or plain seats into stylish, customized designs that make motorcycles look brand new.
